Developmental
Screeners

Do you have concerns with your child's development? Are you unsure if they are meeting their milestones? We can help you identify whether there is a need for further assessment.

Formal
Assessments

Whether you have been referred by another professional, or you are self-referring, we can conduct formal assessment to identify areas for further development.

You-centred
Therapy

Together we can create collaborative goals to truly capture what is important to you. We will then work towards individualised therapy programs and support you in achieving your goals.

We offer:

  • An accessible service that is inclusive of all
  • Therapy for individuals from two years of age through to adulthood
  • Experience with a range of developmental delays and disabilities
  • Evidence-based, individualised therapy approaches
  • Augmentative and Alternative Communication (AAC) knowledge

Developmental areas we can help with:

  • Late or Slow-to-Develop Talkers
  • Unclear Speech
  • Receptive and/or Expressive Language Delays and Disorders
  • Functional Communication including Multimodalities
  • Social Communication Skills
  • Reading and/or Writing Difficulties

We accept:

  • Self-managed and Plan-managed NDIS Participants
  • Clients with Private Health Insurance Coverage
  • Patients with General Practitioner Management Plans
  • Private Pay Clients

Lyn Tulic

BSpPath(Hons), MSPA, CPSP
Lyn graduated with a Bachelor of Speech Pathology (Honours) from the University of Queensland in 2015.
She is a Certified Practicing Member of Speech Pathology Australia.

Lyn has worked as a Speech Pathologist in regional and metropolitan Queensland, as well as abroad in British Columbia, Canada. She has clinical experience supporting young children through to young adults with a range of communication difficulties associated with diagnoses of autism, hearing loss, and other developmental differences. She enjoys holistic intervention inclusive of multimodal communication methods, and has experience with a range of Augmentative and Alternative Communication (AAC) systems.
