Contact us today to learn more about how we can help!

Developmental
Screeners

Do you have concerns with your child's development? Are you unsure if they are meeting their milestones? We can help you identify whether there is a need for further assessment.

Formal
Assessments

Whether you have been referred by another professional, or you are self-referring, we would be happy to conduct formal assessment to identify areas for further development.

You-centred
Therapy

Together we can create collaborative goals to truly capture what is important to you. We will then work towards individualised therapy programs and support you in achieving your goals.

We offer:

  • Therapy for all ages, from birth through to adulthood
  • Consultations for expecting and new parents
  • An environment that is inclusive of all
  • Evidence-based, individualised therapy approaches
  • Service delivery through direct and telepractice models
  • Experience with a range of developmental delays and disabilities
  • Augmentative and Alternative Communication Systems experience

Clinical areas we can help you with:

  • Late or Slow-to-Develop Talkers
  • Unclear or Dysfluent Speech
  • Listening and/or Speaking Communication Breakdowns
  • Social Communication Skills
  • Reading and/or Writing Difficulties
  • Accent Modification
  • Picky Eaters
  • Swallowing Difficulties

We accept:

  • Self-managed and Plan-managed NDIS Participants
  • Clients with Private Health Insurance Coverage
  • Patients with General Practitioner Management Plans
  • Private Pay Clients
